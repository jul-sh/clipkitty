ClipKitty is the clipboard manager that stays simple; even when your clipboard history gets large.

It saves what you copy and makes it easy to find again; text, code, links, colors, images, and files; all in one fast searchable history.

UNLIMITED HISTORY
No small history cap. No short expiration window. ClipKitty keeps your copied items available for as long as you want them.

SEARCH THAT ACTUALLY HELPS
Type what you remember and ClipKitty finds the right item fast, even if the query is incomplete or slightly misspelled.

SEE EVERYTHING BEFORE YOU PASTE
Preview full text, code, images, and colors in one place. No guessing from cut-off snippets.

OPTIONAL ICLOUD SYNC
Sync your clipboard history across devices with iCloud when you want it.

NATURAL KEYBOARD FLOW
Open with Option-Space, search by typing, move with the arrow keys, and press Return to paste.

PRIVATE BY DEFAULT
ClipKitty works on-device with no telemetry, no accounts, and no third-party servers. Optional sync stays in your private iCloud container. Open source and publicly auditable.
